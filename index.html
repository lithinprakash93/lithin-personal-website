About me

Hello, my name is Lithin. I am a freelance UI/UX designer, front-end developer ,branding expert and an artist. I offer design and branding services to a range of businesses; from fashion designers and bloggers, to design agencies and creative professionals.

My products are minimalist yet visually striking, designed to tell a story and showcase the talents of my clients. In a competitive market, it is often the smaller details and visual touches that take your online space to the next level. All my products are designed with impeccable attention to detail and a timeless artistic flair.

Services That I Provide

UI/UX DESIGN

As a skilled UI/UX designer, I create intuitive and visually appealing digital products through user-centered design and use of tools like Figma and AdobeXD.

With experience in user research and usability testing, I strive to craft user-friendly experiences for a variety of projects

WEB DESIGN / DEV.

As a skilled web designer and developer, I have a passion for creating visually stunning static websites. With a strong foundation in design principles and a deep

understanding of HTML, CSS, Java script and other web technologies, I am able to craft professional and effective websites that meet the needs of my clients. I am always seeking to learn and improve, and I am excited to bring my skills and experience to new projects and challenges.

ART

As an artist, I am inspired by the world around me to create vibrant and expressive works of art. Using a variety of mediums, including paint, charcoal,

and digital tools, I strive to capture the beauty and emotion in the people, landscapes, and objects that I depict. I am constantly experimenting and exploring new techniques, and I am excited to share my art with others and see how it touches their lives
